Shopping Guide

Easy steps to complete your purchase

How to Order

If you find a product you're interested in, please contact us through one of the following methods:

📧
Purchase Inquiry Button
On the product detail page, click the "Purchase Inquiry" button. Your email will be pre-filled with the product name and ID.
Browse Products
💬
Contact Form
Use our contact form to send us a message with the product details and any questions you may have.
Contact Us

💡 Response Time

We typically respond within 24-48 hours. Email: contact@verifiedtokyo.com

Payment Methods

  • Credit Card: We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and JCB
  • Bank Transfer: Direct bank transfer available for domestic customers
  • PayPal: Secure payment through PayPal
  • Cash on Delivery: Available for orders within Tokyo area (additional fees may apply)

Secure Payment

All transactions are encrypted and secure. We do not store your credit card information on our servers.

Shipping Information

  • Domestic Shipping (Japan): 2-3 business days via Japan Post or Yamato Transport
  • International Shipping: 5-10 business days depending on destination
  • Express Shipping: Available for urgent orders (additional fees apply)
  • Free Shipping: Orders over ¥100,000 qualify for free domestic shipping

Track Your Order

Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking number via email to monitor your delivery status.
